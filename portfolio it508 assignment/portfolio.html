Our Projects

Smart Dustbin

A smart dustbin is an innovative waste management solution equipped with sensors and internet connectivity. It automatically detects and segregates waste, optimizing disposal and recycling processes. The dustbin can alert sanitation workers when it is full and often includes features like touchless operation and real-time monitoring to enhance hygiene and efficiency.

Smart Car

A smart car crafted with Arduino technology integrates sensors and microcontrollers for autonomous navigation. With ultrasonic sensors for obstacle detection and precise motor control, it maneuvers efficiently. Arduino programming enables remote control and IoT integration, making it an ideal educational platform for robotics enthusiasts to explore autonomous systems and IoT concepts.

Smart Irrigation to Plants

Smart irrigation systems employ sensors and IoT technology to optimize water usage in agriculture. They monitor soil moisture, weather conditions, and plant needs to deliver precise irrigation. With remote access and automated controls, they ensure efficient water management, conserve resources, and enhance crop yield, making them essential tools for sustainable farming practices.

Smart Lock System

A smart lock system utilizes biometric authentication or digital codes for secure access control. Integrated with IoT technology, it offers remote monitoring and management via smartphone apps. With features like keyless entry and activity logs, it enhances security and convenience for homes and businesses, revolutionizing traditional locking mechanisms with advanced digital solutions.
